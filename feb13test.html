Lata Mangeshkar{28 sep 1929 to 06 feb 2022}

  • Lata Mangeshkar comes from a family of performers.
  • She started singing at the age of five.
  • Lata Mangeshkar's 'Aayega Aanewala' song from the movie Mahal in 1949 gave her much-needed stardom.
  • Ghulam Haider was her Godfather and Madan Mohan was her favourite music director.
  • Lata Mangeshkar dedicated 'Ae Mere Watan Ke Logon' to the soldiers who lost their lives in the 1962 war.
  • Lata Mangeshkar became the first-ever Indian to perform live at the iconic Royal Albert Hall.
  • In the 1974 edition of the Guinness Book of Records, she was listed as the most recorded artist, having sung around 25,000 songs.
  • Lata Mangeshkar was an ardent fan of Cricket.
  • She was nominated in 1999 as a Rajya Sabha MP and her term ended in 2006.
  • During her term as an MP, she did not take a single penny or a salary or a house in Delhi.
  • Lata Mangeshkar was the second Indian vocalist to be conferred with Bharat Ratna after Carnatic singer MS Subbalakshmi.
